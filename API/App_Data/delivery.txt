Fast & Reliable Delivery
We offer countrynwide delivery with options for same-day, next-day, and standard shipping. Orders placed before 2 PM are usually shipped the same day. If they’re placed after 2 PM, they’ll usually be shipped the next day.

Estimated Delivery Time
• Same-day delivery: Available in select cities
• Next-day delivery: 1–2 business days
• Standard delivery: 3–5 business days

Delivery Coverage
We deliver to all major cities, towns, and remote areas across the UK. Shipping availability may vary based on product type and location.

Return Policy
Due to the nature of medical products, only unopened, unused items in their original packaging can be returned within 7 days of delivery. Prescription items are non-returnable unless faulty or incorrectly delivered.

Refund Process
Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
